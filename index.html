pixelate

Platform to build your own IoT devices in DIY-way!

Have you ever played Minecraft? Lego? 
Pixelate manifests new format of IoT devices to augment 
reality with 3D-printable modular cubes. Pixelate includes
SDK, DIY kits and real-world examples to get started. You
can build your own pixel and sell it on our platform. 
pixelate-app simplify combination, control and 
orchestration of pixels you have in playful
and intuitive way. Learn, play and collect!
